Tea and Biscuits

Puzzles seen in a new light!  Amuse yourself and your friends with our decorative natural wood puzzles that are not only challenging but quirky and artistic. Puzzles not just to be played with and put away, but to be displayed on a coffee table or shelf.

These unique puzzles are mostly held within a bowl or tray.  Each one is designed and made by ourselves to a high standard of workmanship using as our main tools an electric fretsaw and a wood lathe. Please note these puzzles are not laser cut. A variety of of woods are used allowing their natural colours to produce contrast.
 
In our puzzles we try to produce objects that are unique, fun, highly decorative and tactile.
