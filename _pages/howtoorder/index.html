No two woods are the same and each puzzle made will vary slightly from the photo image on this site or from a puzzle you might have seen at a show.  To compensate for this, I can offer the following service prior to ordering.                 

To preview a puzzle before buying, e-mail us mentioning the item you would like to see and provided the item is in stock, we will by return, e-mail you a photo image. The item in question will be reserved for 48 hours. with no commitment to buy. 

Care and Maintenance
Application of a good quality beeswax polish is recommended from time to time, to the bowls and trays, but not to the puzzle pieces. These can be cleaned with a cloth dampened with white spirit and buffed with a dusting cloth.  Most woods are prone to bleaching, particularly if placed in a sunny position, so avoid siting the puzzles on windowsills or other brightly lit areas. PLEASE NOTE for items that lay on top of other pieces of wood e.g. biscuits, knives, spoons it is a good idea to move them around from time to time to avoid light stains developing.
